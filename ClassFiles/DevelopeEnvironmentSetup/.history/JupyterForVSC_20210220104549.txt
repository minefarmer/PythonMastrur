                    Development environment setup for  VSC

1. Open VSC in the directory that I want to launch Jupyter notebooks
2. jupyter notebook  # Starts the notebook server

3. Creating a new notebook.
    a. Click on the NEW button.
    b. Click on Python3.
    c. Click on Untitled.
    d. Enter title of the new notebook.
    e. Click on Jupyter icon.
    f. Go to running tab and click.  # It will show which file is running.

23. Use Control-C to shut down the notebook server.
